TextOracle AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Summary: TextOracle AI does not collect, store or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

1. Overview

TextOracle AI ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension developed by IA Squared Extensions. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

TextOracle AI does not:

  • Collect or transmit your personal information to any server operated by us
  • Track your browsing history or behaviour across websites
  • Store the text you type or have checked
  • Share any data with third parties for advertising or analytics
  • Use cookies or any form of tracking technology

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device:

  • Settings: Your preferences such as language, tone, theme and feature toggles
  • API Key: If you choose to provide your own Anthropic or OpenAI API key, it is stored locally on your device only
  • License Key: Your Lemon Squeezy Pro license key, stored locally for activation verification
  • Snippet Library: Prompts and templates you save are stored locally
  • Writing Memory: Accepted and rejected correction patterns you have personalised, stored locally
  • Usage Counts: Daily AI check counts to enforce free plan limits, stored locally

4. AI API Calls

When you use AI features (grammar check, tone rewriting, translation, AI composer), the text you submit is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider:

  • Anthropic Claude API (if you use Anthropic) — governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI API (if you use OpenAI) — governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy

These calls are made directly from your browser using your own API key. IA Squared Extensions does not see, intercept or store the content of these requests.

5. License Activation

When you activate a Pro license, the extension communicates with Lemon Squeezy's licensing API (lemonsqueezy.com) to validate your license key. This sends only your license key to Lemon Squeezy for verification. Their handling of this data is governed by Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy.

6. Permissions Used

TextOracle AI requests the following browser permissions:

  • storage: To save your settings and preferences locally on your device
  • tabs: To detect which platform you are using (Gmail, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.) so platform-specific writing rules can be applied
  • host_permissions (all URLs): To inject the writing assistant into any web page where you type, since users write on many different websites

7. Children's Privacy

TextOracle AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the Chrome Web Store update mechanism. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
